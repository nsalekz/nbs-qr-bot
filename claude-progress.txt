NBS IPS QR Code Generator + Telegram Bot
==========================================
Status: Working / Complete
Last updated: 09/04/26

Rust CLI + Telegram bot that generates NBS IPS QR codes for Serbian bank payments.
Parses raw invoice/payment text dumps and auto-extracts account, amount, payee name.

Telegram bot: @QR_NBS_bot

Usage:
  CLI:  nbs-qr-bot gen "paste invoice text here" -o output.png
  Bot:  nbs-qr-bot bot --token <TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN>
        or set TELOXIDE_TOKEN env var

Key spec details:
- Amount (tag I) MUST have trailing comma even for whole numbers (e.g. RSD1300,)
- Newlines in N/P fields must be real CR+LF (0x0D 0x0A)
- Payment purpose (tag S) max 35 chars
- Account numbers are 18 digits, no hyphens
